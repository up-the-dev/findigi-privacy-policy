Privacy Policy for Findigi Mobile Banking

Last Updated: April 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Findigi ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and securing your financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Findigi mobile banking application ("the App").

By using Findigi, you agree to the terms of this policy. If you disagree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal & Financial Data

  • Full name, date of birth, and government-issued ID (e.g., NIN, passport)
  • Bank account numbers, transaction history, and card details
  • Contact information (email, phone number, residential address)

b. Device & Security Data

  • Device ID, IMEI, and SIM information (via READ_PHONE_STATE permission)
  • IP address, operating system, and mobile network
  • Biometric data (if used for authentication)

Purpose: Fraud prevention, securing login attempts, and complying with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations.

c. App Usage & Analytics

  • Crash reports and performance logs
  • User behavior (e.g., screens visited, session duration)

3. How We Use Your Data

Purpose Examples
Identity Verification Confirming your identity during registration or transactions.
Fraud Prevention Detecting suspicious login attempts using device data.
Transaction Processing Executing transfers, bill payments, or withdrawals.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • Banks & Payment Processors (to complete transactions)
  • Regulatory Bodies (if required by law)
  • Security Services (e.g., fraud detection tools)

5. Data Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Access Controls: Strict internal policies limit employee access.
  • Regular Audits: Penetration testing and security reviews.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data (via in-app settings or email request).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Disable permissions (e.g., READ_PHONE_STATE) via device settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of updates via:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email to registered accounts
